Scoring Methodology

Dimension weights and assessment criteria · 1–5 scale · 1 = very poor fit, 3 = adequate, 5 = exceptional
Ease of Use 20% Onboarding speed and daily UX for non-technical sales reps; includes mobile app quality
Integration 20% Pre-built connectors to existing stack — Slack, ERP, email, calendar, and marketing automation
Pricing 20% Total cost of ownership for 50-seat deployment over 3 years, including implementation and add-ons
Support 15% SLA quality, availability of a dedicated customer success manager, and response time benchmarks
Customization 15% Custom objects and fields, workflow automation builder depth, and public API quality
Data Privacy 10% SOC 2 Type II certification, GDPR compliance posture, and EU data residency options
