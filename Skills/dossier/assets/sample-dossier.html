Quick Take

Notion's enterprise push is real — SSO, audit logs, and DLP close the last gap that kept enterprise buyers away. Watch their Q3 2026 enterprise deal flow closely.

Overview

Notion is a connected workspace platform that combines documents, wikis, and relational databases into a single flexible canvas. Founded in 2016 by Ivan Zhao and Simon Last, the company grew through aggressive product-led growth to an estimated $300M ARR by early 2026, making it one of the most prominent names in the modern productivity stack. Their core innovation — treating every piece of content as a database block — enables non-technical users to build lightweight apps without code, which positions Notion directly against both traditional project management tools and low-code platforms. The November 2025 launch of Notion for Enterprise with SSO, audit logging, and DLP controls closes the last major gap that prevented security-conscious enterprise buyers from standardizing on the platform, making Q2 and Q3 2026 the most important selling windows for competitive displacement. Notion AI 2.0's multi-page context window is the most capable document-native AI in the market as of May 2026 and is driving meaningful upgrade conversions in their mid-market segment.
