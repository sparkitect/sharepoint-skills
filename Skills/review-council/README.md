# Review Council

Convenes a panel of seven expert AI personas to review, stress-test, and improve any SharePoint artefact — a Word document in a library, a news post, a site page, a list-item description, an Excel workbook, a PowerPoint deck, a PDF, or even a half-formed idea typed into chat — then delivers a structured verdict with specific, actionable fixes.

This is not a proofreader. The Council exists to surface what the author can't see: blind spots, unstated assumptions, structural weaknesses, audience misalignment, and missed opportunities. It works on anything from a polished board deck stored in a SharePoint library to a half-formed idea spoken aloud.

![preview](./assets/preview.png)

## What you get

- **Seven distinct perspectives** — The Adversary, Audience Proxy, Architect, Analyst, Storyteller, Operator, and Strategist each review your content independently, then respond to each other by name
- **A structured verdict** — ASSESSMENT, RATING (Ready to Ship / Almost There / Needs Work / Rethink), CONFIDENCE score, TOP 3 ISSUES, TOP 3 STRENGTHS, and 5 CONCRETE FIXES in priority order
- **Content-type-aware scrutiny** — different personas lead for Word docs, Excel workbooks, PowerPoint decks, PDFs stored in libraries, SharePoint pages and news posts, list-item descriptions, and raw ideas
- **A MINORITY REPORT** — the strongest dissent that almost changed the outcome, so you know what you're overriding

## When to use

Ask Copilot, with the SharePoint artefact open or referenced:

- *"review this"* / *"critique this"* / *"poke holes in this"* / *"red team this"*
- *"what am I missing"* / *"would this work"* / *"is this any good"*
- *"convene the council"* / *"panel review"* / *"get perspectives on this"*
- *"stress-test this proposal"* / *"review this news post before I publish"*

Works on a file open in the library, a SharePoint page or news post, a list item's description field, an uploaded `.docx` / `.xlsx` / `.pptx` / `.pdf`, pasted text, or a verbally described idea.

> **Confidentiality.** The Council reviews content in place — it does not copy content out of SharePoint. If the artefact is sensitive (HR, legal, M&A, personal data), tell Copilot once at the start; the Council will summarise feedback in general terms and keep specific quotes/figures to a minimum in its synthesis.
